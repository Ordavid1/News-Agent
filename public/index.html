FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is News Agent?
News Agent is an AI-powered social media automation platform that automatically generates and publishes trending news content to your social media accounts including Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit, and Facebook. It saves content creators up to 95% of their time by handling content creation and posting automatically.
How does AI social media automation work?
News Agent uses advanced AI to monitor trending news topics, automatically generate engaging social media posts tailored for each platform, and schedule them for optimal posting times. The AI understands platform-specific requirements like Twitter's character limits and LinkedIn's professional tone.
Which social media platforms are supported?
News Agent supports Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Reddit, Facebook, Telegram, and Instagram. Depending on your subscription plan, you can post to multiple platforms simultaneously with content optimized for each platform's unique requirements.
How much does News Agent cost?
News Agent offers four pricing tiers: Starter at $49/month (10 posts/day, 5 platforms), Growth at $149/month (20 posts/day, 7 platforms), Professional at $399/month (30 posts/day, 7 platforms), and Business at $799/month (45 posts/day, all 9 platforms). A free trial is available.
Can I try News Agent for free?
Yes! News Agent offers a free tier with 1 post per week so you can test the AI content generation and see how it works for your social media strategy before committing to a paid plan.
