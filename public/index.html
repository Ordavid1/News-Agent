FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Configure News?
Configure News is an AI-powered social media automation platform with two engines: News Agents that automatically generate and publish trending content across 10 platforms, and Marketing Pro that trains AI on your brand voice, manages Meta Ads campaigns, and generates on-brand media. It saves content creators up to 95% of their time by handling content creation, publishing, and advertising automatically.
How does AI social media automation work?
Configure News uses advanced AI to monitor trending news topics, automatically generate engaging social media posts tailored for each platform, and schedule them for optimal posting times. The AI understands platform-specific requirements like Twitter's character limits, LinkedIn's professional tone, TikTok's viral caption style, and YouTube Shorts formatting.
Which social media platforms are supported?
Configure News supports 10 platforms: Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Reddit, Facebook, Instagram, Threads, Telegram, WhatsApp, TikTok, and YouTube. All paid plans include all 10 platforms. Content is automatically optimized for each platform's unique requirements — character limits, tone, media formats, and community guidelines.
How much does Configure News cost?
Configure News offers four pricing tiers: Free at $0/month (1 post/week, 1 AI agent), Starter at $25/month (6 posts/day, 2 videos/month), Growth at $75/month (12 posts/day, 10 videos/month), and Business at $250/month (30 posts/day, 30 videos/month). All paid plans include all 10 platforms. Optional add-ons include Marketing Pro+ at $19/month for Meta Ads management.
Can I try Configure News for free?
Yes! Configure News offers a free tier with 1 post per week so you can test the AI content generation and see how it works for your social media strategy before committing to a paid plan.
What is Brand Voice and how does it work?
Brand Voice is Configure News's AI training feature that learns your unique writing style and brand guidelines. Once trained, it generates content that consistently matches your brand's tone, vocabulary, and messaging across all 10 supported platforms, ensuring every post feels authentically yours. It uses LoRA training for visual brand consistency as well.
What is Marketing Pro+ and what does it include?
Marketing Pro+ is an optional add-on at $19/month per ad account that gives you full Meta Ads campaign management for Facebook and Instagram. It includes post boosting with configurable budgets, auto-boost rules triggered by engagement thresholds, reusable audience templates with demographic and interest targeting, reach estimation, and a dedicated marketing analytics dashboard with spend and ROI tracking.
Does Configure News support TikTok and YouTube video creation?
Yes. Configure News includes AI video generation powered by Flux2-Pro for creating short-form video content optimized for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels. The platform features an intelligent video prompt engine and automatic thumbnail generation. Video quotas vary by tier: Starter gets 2 videos/month, Growth gets 10, and Business gets 30.
What analytics and reporting does Configure News offer?
Configure News provides a unified analytics dashboard covering platform performance (per-platform engagement, reach, and growth), agent performance (which AI agents are performing best), and content and timing insights (what types of posts and what posting times drive the most engagement). Business tier subscribers can export all data to CSV for external reporting.
What is the Brand Kit / Media feature?
Brand Kit lets you train AI on your brand's visual assets — logos, colors, imagery style. The system then generates on-brand images and media for your posts. It also supports creating Playable Ads for interactive mobile ad experiences and uses LoRA training to maintain visual consistency across all generated content.
