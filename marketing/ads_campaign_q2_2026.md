### YouTube Ad #1 — "I Automated ALL My Social Media"
**Segment A** | Community Managers & SMBs | Autonomous News Agent

**Format:** 45-60 seconds | Landscape 16:9 | Skippable pre-roll or in-feed discovery
**Tone:** Relatable, testimonial-style, conversational authority
**Audio:** Voiceover (warm, confident voice) + subtle background music

#### Script

---

**[0:00-0:05] — HOOK (must grab before "Skip Ad")**

*Visual:* Person sitting at desk surrounded by multiple screens — each showing a different social media platform. They look overwhelmed, rubbing their temples.

**Voiceover:** "I used to spend four hours a day posting on social media. Finding content, writing posts, formatting for every platform... Now I spend zero."

---

**[0:05-0:12] — THE SETUP**

*Visual:* Screen transitions to the Configure News dashboard. Clean, professional interface.

**Voiceover:** "I found this tool called Configure News. It has these things called AI Agents — and they changed everything."

*Visual:* Cursor clicks "Create New Agent" button.

---

**[0:12-0:25] — THE THREE STEPS**

*Visual:* Step-by-step screen recording with animated callouts:

**Voiceover:** "Step one — connect your platforms."

*Visual:* 10 platform icons light up one by one: Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Reddit, Facebook, Threads, Telegram, WhatsApp.

**Voiceover:** "Step two — tell your agent what to talk about."

*Visual:* Agent config: Topics field shows "Artificial Intelligence, Startup News, Tech Trends". Tone selector: "Professional". Posts per day slider moves to "12".

**Voiceover:** "Step three — flip the switch."

*Visual:* Automation toggle slides to ON. Green pulse animation.

---

**[0:25-0:40] — THE PAYOFF**

*Visual:* Time-lapse montage — posts appearing in real-time across all 10 platforms. Each post is uniquely formatted for its platform (short + hashtags on Twitter, long-form on LinkedIn, visual on Instagram, video on TikTok). Counter in corner ticks up: 1... 5... 12... 24 posts.

**Voiceover:** "It finds trending news in your niche. Writes content optimized for each platform. And posts — automatically. 24/7. While you sleep, while you're in meetings, while you're actually running your business."

*Visual:* Person from opening shot is now at a coffee shop, relaxed. Phone notification: "12 posts published today across 10 platforms."

---

**[0:40-0:50] — THE CLOSE**

*Visual:* Dashboard stats: "This week: 84 posts published. Time saved: 28 hours." Pricing badge slides in: "Free forever plan available. Paid starts at $25/month."

**Voiceover:** "Your first agent is completely free. Set it up in under five minutes. And if you want to scale? Thirty posts a day starts at twenty-five bucks a month. That's less than one hour of a freelancer."

---

**[0:50-0:58] — CTA**

*Visual:* Logo animation. URL: configurenews.com. "Start Free" button animation.

**Voiceover:** "Configure News. Set up your free AI agent right now. Link in the description."

*Visual:* End screen with subscribe button and video card.

---

#### Production Notes
- **Filming options:** (A) Real person talking head + screen recordings, or (B) Pure screen recording with professional voiceover
- **Critical:** The first 5 seconds must hook before "Skip Ad" — the overwhelmed-desk shot + "four hours a day" stat is the anchor
- **Screen recordings:** Capture from live dashboard with real data — don't mock up fake interfaces
- **Thumbnail:** Split image — left: person stressed at desk (red tint), right: person relaxed with phone (green tint). Text: "I Automated ALL My Social Media"
- **End screen:** Include subscribe prompt + link card to landing page

### YouTube Ad #2 — "The Agency Secret Weapon"
**Segment B** | Agencies & Solo Marketers | Marketing Pro

**Format:** 60-90 seconds | Landscape 16:9 | Skippable pre-roll or in-feed discovery
**Tone:** Premium, authoritative, "insider knowledge" — speaks to marketing professionals
**Audio:** Voiceover (sharp, confident) + cinematic underscore

#### Script

---

**[0:00-0:05] — HOOK**

*Visual:* Close-up of AI-generated content on a screen — generic, bland, obviously AI. Red X stamps appear on each piece.

**Voiceover:** "Every agency's dirty secret? Their 'AI-powered content' all sounds the same. Because it is. What if your AI actually sounded like YOUR brand?"

---

**[0:05-0:08] — TRANSITION**

*Visual:* Screen wipes to the Configure News Marketing Pro tab. Premium interface, dark mode.

**Voiceover:** "This is Marketing Pro. Three AI tools that agencies are quietly using to outproduce their competition."

---

**[0:08-0:25] — BRAND VOICE**

*Visual:* Brand Voice tab walkthrough:
1. "Analyze My Posts" button → progress bar: "Analyzing 347 posts across 6 platforms..."
2. Voice Profile card appears: tone descriptors, vocabulary patterns, style markers
3. "Generate Content" → new post appears
4. **Side-by-side comparison:** Original human post (left) vs. AI-generated post (right). Highlight: identical tone, matching vocabulary, same energy.

**Voiceover:** "Tool one — Brand Voice. Feed it your past posts from any platform. It doesn't just summarize your style — it learns it. Your vocabulary. Your rhythm. Your personality. Then it generates new content that's indistinguishable from what you'd write yourself. Not generic. Not templated. Yours."

---

**[0:25-0:42] — BRAND ASSETS**

*Visual:* Brand Assets tab walkthrough:
1. Upload grid: 15 brand images dragged in
2. "Train Model" → progress animation: "Training custom AI model on your brand..."
3. Brand Kit extraction: color palette swatches (actual hex codes), extracted logos (clean cutouts), detected personas, background graphics
4. "Generate" → type prompt "product launch announcement graphic" → stunning on-brand image renders

**Voiceover:** "Tool two — Brand Assets. Upload your brand imagery. The AI trains a custom model — learning your colors, your style, your visual DNA. It extracts your entire brand kit automatically. Then generates new visuals that look like your designer made them. Every time. On brand. On demand."

---

**[0:42-0:62] — PLAYABLE ADS (THE WOW MOMENT)**

*Visual:* Playable Ads tab walkthrough:
1. Select game type: "Catch the Falling Items"
2. Brand Kit auto-populates: logos become falling items, brand colors fill the background
3. Preview window: an actual playable mini-game running — products falling, player catching them with a branded basket, score counting up, brand logo everywhere
4. End screen: CTA button with custom URL
5. Flash: "Deploy to Google Ads | Meta Ads | Unity Ads"
6. Stat graphic: "Playable ads: 10-50x higher engagement than static ads"

**Voiceover:** "And tool three — this is the one that changes everything. Playable Ads. Your brand kit turns into an interactive mini-game. Your logos, your colors, your products — inside a real, playable experience. Catch-the-product games. Logo challenges. Brand stories. Deploy them straight to Google Ads, Meta, or Unity. And playable ads? They don't just get seen. They get played. We're talking ten to fifty times more engagement than static ads."

---

**[0:62-0:72] — THE CLOSE**

*Visual:* Quick montage: Brand Voice post publishing → Brand Asset image in a campaign → Playable ad running on a phone. Three icons converge into the Configure News logo.

**Voiceover:** "Brand Voice. Brand Assets. Playable Ads. Three tools. One platform. Built for agencies and solo marketers who need to punch above their weight."

---

**[0:72-0:82] — CTA**

*Visual:* Logo + URL. "Try Marketing Pro" button. Pricing: "Start exploring free at configurenews.com"

**Voiceover:** "Try Marketing Pro at Configure News dot com. Your brand deserves better than templates. Link in the description."

*Visual:* End screen with subscribe + video card.

#### Production Notes
- **Key moment:** The playable ad demo MUST show an actual game running — this is the "holy sh*t" moment. Record a real playable from the dashboard.
- **The side-by-side brand voice comparison** is the credibility proof — spend time making this look real and impressive
- **Thumbnail:** Three glowing icons (microphone for Voice, paintbrush for Assets, game controller for Playable) converging into a lightning bolt. Text: "The Agency Secret Weapon"
- **Pacing:** This ad is longer — use cinematic transitions and a building musical score to maintain attention
- **B-roll option:** Intercut with shots of marketers/agency teams reacting positively to results

## CAMPAIGN SETUP RECOMMENDATIONS

### TikTok Ads Manager
- **Objective:** Traffic or Conversions (website visits / sign-ups)
- **Audience targeting:**
  - Ad #1: Interest in "Social Media Marketing", "Community Management", "Small Business", "Content Marketing" | Age 25-45
  - Ad #2: Interest in "Digital Marketing", "Marketing Agency", "Brand Management", "Advertising" | Age 25-50
- **Budget:** Start $20-50/day per ad, optimize after 3-5 days of data
- **Placements:** TikTok feed + TikTok Search

### YouTube Ads
- **Campaign type:** Video — skippable in-stream + in-feed discovery
- **Audience targeting:**
  - Ad #1: In-market for "Social Media Management Software", "Marketing Automation" | Custom intent: "social media automation", "schedule posts"
  - Ad #2: In-market for "Advertising & Marketing Services", "Digital Marketing Software" | Custom intent: "AI marketing tool", "brand content generator", "playable ads"
- **Budget:** Start $30-75/day per ad
- **Bidding:** Target CPA or Maximize conversions

### Google Ads
- **Campaign type:** Search — RSA
- **Match types:** Start with phrase match, expand to broad with smart bidding after conversion data
- **Budget:** Start $25-50/day per campaign
- **Bidding:** Start with Maximize clicks, switch to Target CPA after 30+ conversions
- **Ad extensions:** Sitelinks (above), callout extensions ("Free Plan Available", "10 Platforms", "AI-Powered"), structured snippets (Platforms: Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Reddit)
